What an incredible weekend at **NexHacks 2026**! I'm thrilled to share that my team, **Team Swish**, took home the **$2000 First Place Grand Prize** and the **Best UI/UX Award**!

![Team Swish winning first place](/nexhacks-2026-win.jpg)

## The Project: Swish

We built **Swish**, an AI-powered basketball training assistant that uses computer vision to analyze your shooting form in real-time. It provides instant feedback on shot arc, release point, and body alignment, helping players of all levels improve their game without needing an expensive personal coach.

### Key Features
- **Real-time Shot Tracking:** Using a standard webcam, Swish tracks the basketball's trajectory and whether it goes in the hoop.
- **Form Analysis:** Pose estimation algorithms analyze the player's shooting mechanics.
- **Voice Feedback:** The system gives audible corrections (e.g., "Higher arc", "Elbow in").

## The Experience

It was a grueling 36 hours of coding, debugging, and caffeine, but the energy in the room was electric. We faced some major challenges with getting the real-time inference to run smoothly on edge devices, but optimizing our TensorFlow Lite models paid off in the end.

A huge shoutout to the organizers for putting on such a great event, and to my amazing teammates! We're planning to continue working on Swish and see how far we can take it.
